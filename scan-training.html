Scan Training Guide

How to Use the Scanning Stations

SDC uses QR code labels on product cases. Each label encodes the SDC SKU, product name, and description. Use the barcode scanner or copy/paste the text codes below to practice.

  1. 1Open the Scan Receiving Station (green) to log incoming shipments, or Scan Out Station (red) to log sold items
  2. 2Name your session (e.g., "Truck delivery 4/4" or "Sales 4/4")
  3. 3Point the barcode scanner at a QR label — or paste a text code and press Enter
  4. 4You'll hear a beep and see a flash confirming each scan
  5. 5Check the Summary tab for totals, and History for past sessions
  6. 6Use Export CSV to download records for backup

Tips & Troubleshooting

  • Make sure the scan input field is focused (click on it) before scanning
  • The status dot turns green (receive) or red (scan out) when the scanner is ready
  • If you get a red flash, the QR code format wasn't recognized — check the label
  • Each scan in Receiving adds 1 unit; each scan in Scan Out removes 1 unit as "sold"
  • The Inventory Dashboard shows the combined stock: Received minus Removed
  • Always export CSV at the end of a session for backup
  • Sessions are saved in the browser — clearing browser data will erase scan history
